Modal Auxilliary


Modal auxilliary are such a auxilliary in english which modifies the meaning of the given sentences. There are such a helping verb in english which gives some special implication or some special meaning to the given sentences. But keep in mind that we must use first form of the verb after a modal auxilliary. Some of the important modal auxilliary in english are as follows:


  1. Can
  2. May
  3. Will
  4. Should
  5. Must


Can :

It is used to show capacity, ability and permission.
  1. I can speak Marathi, Hindi and English.
  2. You can solve this problem.
  3. She cannot miss the opportunities.
  4. Can you give me the notes?


May :

It is used for showing possibility, uncertainity/less certainity and permission.
  1. It may be rain or may not be tonight.
  2. She may be come first in our college.
  3. You may appear for any entrance exam.
  4. She may be late for her classes.

Will :

It is used to show futurity, ambition, dream, plan, very polite request and habitual action in present tense.
  1. We will go for picnic.
  2. Will you come with me to office.
  3. He will disturb the class.
  4. She will come first in our college.

Should :

Should shows responsibility and moral duty.
  1. We should respect to our parents and teachers.
  2. We should help to the poor person.
  3. I should be careful about my studies.
  4. They should submit the project before the due date.

Must :

It is used to show compulsion and obligation.
  1. We must carry hall ticket in examination hall.
  2. They must appear for annual practical and oral examination examination.
  3. I must submit my examination form.
  4. You must study hard for best marks.
